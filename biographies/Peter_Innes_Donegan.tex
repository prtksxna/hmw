\biohead{Peter Innes Donegan}{}\index{Donegan, Peter Innes}

The eldest of three children of \bioref{Ernest_Albert_Donegan} and \bioref{Lynda_Amy_Kathleen_Innes},
born on 5 June 1913.

He married \bioref{Helen_Margaret_Hall} on 15 September 1936 in St Georges Cathedral in \idx{Perth}.\cite{MargPeterMarriage}
They had one daughter, in 1938.\cite{JHBirthNotice}
He died in an aviation accident on 18 January 1939 in \idx{Katherine} in the \idx{Northern Territory}.
