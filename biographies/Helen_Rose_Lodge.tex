Helen Rose Lodge was born on April 3 1888 in Grass Valley (near \idx{Northam})\cite{FamilyNotices} to Thomas Soutter Lodge (\p{Thomas_Soutter_Lodge}) and Sarah Constance Lodge (n\'{e}e Leake, \p{Sarah_Constance_Leake}).\cite{BMD}

She married Harold Aubrey Hall (\p{Harold_Aubrey_Hall}) and had three daughters: Margaret, Joan, and Connie.
