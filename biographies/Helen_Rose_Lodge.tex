Helen Rose Lodge was born in \id{Northam} 1888 to Thomas Soutter Lodge (\p{Thomas_Soutter_Lodge}) and Sarah Constance Lodge (n\'{e}e Leake, \p{Sarah_Constance_Leake}).\citeref{BMD}

She married Harold Aubrey Hall and had three daughters: Margaret, Joan, and Connie.
