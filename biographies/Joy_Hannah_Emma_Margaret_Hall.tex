\biohead{Joy Hannah Emma Margaret Hall}{}

Joy Hannah Emma Margaret Hall was born in 1876, fourth child of \bioref{Hannah_Boyd_Lazenby} and \bioref{William_Shakespeare_Hall}. She married \bioref{Gervase_Clifton} around 1900 and they had four daughters:
\bioref{Millicent_Joyce_Branson_Clifton} (who died young);
\bioref{Lily_Clifton};
\bioref{Madge_Louise_Clifton}; and
\bioref{Tita_Clifton}.

They all attended Perth College.\cite{WestAusSocialNotes1913}

She died in 1960.
