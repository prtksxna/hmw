\biohead{William Murray Wilson}{WilliamMurrayWilson}{}

For his service during the \id{Second World War}, Murray was awarded\citeref{RMWDeptAirLetter} four campaign stars (1939--45 Star, Atlantic Star, Africa Star \& Clasp, and Pacific Star) and three medals (Defence Medal, War Medal 1939--45, and Australia Service Medal 1939-45).
