Sarah Constance Lodge (n\'{e}e Leake) was born on 1 February 1860. She married Thomas Soutter Lodge (\p{Thomas_Soutter_Lodge}) on the 15\textsuperscript{th} of September 1886 (her granddaughter Helen Margaret Wilson (\p{Helen_Margaret_Lodge}) married her first husband Peter James Donegan exactly fifty years later, to the day\cite{HMWnote}).
