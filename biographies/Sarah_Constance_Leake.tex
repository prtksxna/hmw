Sarah Constance Lodge (n\'{e}e Leake) married Thomas Soutter Lodge (\p{Thomas_Soutter_Lodge}) on the 15\textsuperscript{th} of September 1886 (her granddaughter Helen Margaret Wilson (\p{Helen_Margaret_Lodge}) married her first husband Peter James Donegan exactly fifty years later, to the day\citeref{1}). 

Known as Constance (and Gag by her grandchildren).
